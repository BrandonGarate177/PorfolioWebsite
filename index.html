    Current Passion Project

    One of my closest friends is a Math Tutor. The two of us had a talk about how the current math curriculum often stifles the natural curiosity and excitement for learning mathematics. This conversation was heavily inspired by "A Mathematicians Lament" - Paul Lockhart. Math should be taught with a heavy emphasis on curiosty. We decided that we would create our own paper arguing the importance of curiosity in the K-12 Math Curriculum. We are working towards getting a 4 week after school program where we would teach students with a new approach, with the goal to submit a paper arguing for a revision on the way mathematics should be taught. Math is an art, much like how music is an art. If you were to give students years of homework deciphering sheet music, the passion and curiosty they once help for music would dwindle.
